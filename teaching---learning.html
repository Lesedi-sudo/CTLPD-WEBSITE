Welcome to the Centre for teaching, Learning and Programme Development.

 

 

The Sol Plaatje University Centre for Teaching, Learning and Programme Development (CTLPD)

 

Was established in 1th July 2017. When Dr Nhlahla Mpofu was appointed SPU's first Senior Manager.

CTLPD provides support to Sol Plaatje University's

Academic Support Programme; Academic project in the following areas; Academic Literacy and Life-long Learning;

Innovation Digital Pedagogy; Programme and Curriculum Design; Quality Assurance of Teaching learning;

Academic Career Path Development and Learning Analytics. SPU uses Moodle, making this platform accessible

to both students and academic staff members through the Moodle platform.

This platform is used for students and staff training and development.

 

Student Academic Support

 

The Centre for Teaching, Learning and Programme Development (CTLPD) provides from Admission to Gradutation Student Academic Support (AGSAS) programme. AGSAS programme ensures that students have adequate information to make informed career choices from admissions, during registrations and while on-cource to graduation. On-course support programme monitors and evaluates student's academic performance and enhances student potential to succeed. To achieve the foregoing, the Centre runs the following programmes:

 

 

 

Academic and digital literacy Support

Tutorship Support Support

Student Academic Advising Support
