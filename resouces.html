E-LEARNING PROGRAMME

 

This portfolio ensures that the academic programmes are deepened highest-quality learning, teaching, curricula, research

and administration in the university through the development, implementation and continuous improvement of quality,

quality improvement and quality enhancement in university, and to ensure that the highest quality systems

and procedures to achieve these operate.

 

The e-learning programme offered by the CTLPD aims to support academics and researchers

in the effective use of the Learning Management System (LMS), and staff members are encouraged

to attend scheduled and informal workshops to ensure the optimal use of LMS for teaching and learning activities.

Furthermore, this portfolio will ensure that seminars and workshops are offered on specific technologies and their affordances,

to support teaching and learning and assist academics in their teaching practice with the focus on students and their needs.

The CTLPD will deliver a strategic support service to lecturers,

researchers and students in the effective pedagogical use of networked

computer technologies for teaching and learning. Staff at CTLPD will provide training in the use of the LMS and assist lecturers,

researchers and students in designing online teaching and learning material.

The system enables lecturers to facilitate the learning process by applying various modes of online teaching.

The traditional face-to-face classroom activity is enhanced with synchronous and asynchronous activities.

implements the Career Path Development Programme in conjunction with the Research Office.

their careers in higher education. Taking cognisance of and in alignment with the University Capacity

Development Programme (UCDP), and Staffing South Africaâ€™s Universities Framework (SSAUF)
