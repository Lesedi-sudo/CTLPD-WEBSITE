Student Academic Advising Support

 

Student academic advising programme of Sol Plaatje University creates opportunities for students (advisees)

and CTLPD academic advisers to collaboratively engage in a process of helping an advisee to understand

his/her learning path and set personal academic goals. Furthermore, provide students with appropriate

continuous academic support towards student success.

 

 

Student Academic Support Student

 

The Centre for Teaching, Learning and Programme Development (CTLPD) provides from Admission to Gradutation Student Academic Support (AGSAS) programme. AGSAS programme ensures that students have adequate information to make informed career choices from admissions, during registrations and while on-cource to graduation. On-course support programme monitors and evaluates student's academic performance and enhances student potential to succeed. To achieve the foregoing, the Centre runs the following programmes:

 

 

 

Academic and digital literacy Support

 

Through academic literacy programme the CTLPD support students to be able to discuss and analyze formal

academic texts and use subject-specific jargon/ discipline-specific discourse of knowledge to be able

to productively contribute to ongoing conversations within specific academic fields.

 

 

Tutorship Support Support

 

The tutorship support programme offered by the CTLPD aimed to provide on-course student academic support.

In so doing, the CTLPD creates one-to-one or small groups interactions facilitated by a tutor who acts as the

more knowledgeable other (MKO) with expertise in a specific content area or discipline.

Tutors assist students individually or in small groups and provide a variety of services from study skills,

note-taking strategies and often help students review material from class.
